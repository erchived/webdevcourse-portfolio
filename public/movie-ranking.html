Top 5 Best Dishes According to Me

Instead of creating Movie Ranking Project, I want to twist it into Food Ranking Project

Any Kind of Sambal 🥵

(Bawang, Tomat, Terasi, Green Chili—You Name It!)
The ultimate sidekick! Sambal makes everything better—-grilled fish, fried chicken, crispy tofu… it's basically the soul of Sundanese food.
Go-to spots: Warung Spesial Sambal, Sambal Bakar, Jeletot.

Grilled Suan Cai Yu or Pickled Veggie Fish 🐟🔥

This Chinese dish hits different. You can get it soupy or grilled, but grilled with a kick of mala or Sichuan sauce?
Go-to spots: Ban Tian Yao, Chi Yu Ba (budget-friendly version!)

Bakmi 🍜

Noodles with all the good stuff—chicken, pork, or fish balls. My fav version? Thin, slightly chewy noodles that don't fight back when you chew.
Go-to spots: Bakmi Aling, Bakmi Garing Awei, Bakmi Ono.

Pho-nomenal Pho 🌿🥣

A warm bowl of Vietnamese comfort—fragrant herbs, clear broth, and smooth rice noodles. Perfect for rainy days or when you're feeling meh.
Go-to spots: Pho Baba, Pho Minh.

Japanese Food 🇯🇵🍣

Let's be real—Japanese food is top-tier. Ramen, sushi, curry, nikudon, takoyaki, okonomiyaki, soba... it’s impossible to pick just one!
Go-to spots: Japan itself (lol) but also Marugame Udon, Obihiro Nikudon.
